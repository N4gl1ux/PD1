What is Synthwave?

Synthwave (also called retrowave, futuresynth) is a subgenre of an electronic music that is stylistically influenced by 80's and 90's science-fiction movies and video games rather than 80's pop music songs at that time.

A term, often used by this genre is Retrofuturism which shows the future of technology but depicted from an earlier time period.
